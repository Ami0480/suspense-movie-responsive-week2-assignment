Why suspence?

Why do you like the supense movies?

The suspense movies keep me on the edge of my seat, making me curious and excited to find out what happens next. The tension and unexpected twists make the experience thrilling and engaging. They create a powerful mix of curiosity and tension—my mind is constantly trying to piece together clues, predict what is coming next, and figure out the truth. That mental challenge, combined with the emotional rush of unexpected twists or close calls, makes the whole experience thrilling and addictive. It is like being part of a puzzle that keeps unfolding in real time.
